F.A.Q

Arcadium GOACT is an Hourglass-style game which implements the GOACT token on TRON. As players buy and sell GD1VS, the price and value incrementally rises and falls.

10% of every Deposit and Withdrawal is collected as a "Stability Fee" which makes up the Dividends that players earn, as GD1VS are bought and sold.

When you hold GD1VS, you are automatically earning Dividends from other players who are buying and selling GD1VS also.
Whenever you'd like, you can Compound your Dividends, which will get your more GD1VS!
You can also Withdraw your Dividends as GOACT, straight to your wallet.

The best way to earn more GD1VS is to Compound the Dividends you earn from others, and to Refer others to join in!
If you click on "Referrals" in the Game Menu, you will be able to share your Referral Link.
When others use it to buy GD1VS, you get a bonus!

If you no longer wish to play Arcadium GOACT, that is 100% okay!
All you have to do is sell all of your GD1VS, then Withdraw them from your Dividends.
You will receive the resulting GOACT in your wallet, and you're free to go!

How to Play

When you buy GD1VS with GOACT, a 10% fee is applied to your deposit. The same applies for every other player, on every deposit. This fee makes up part of the "Dividends" which are paid to those who already hold GD1VS. As this goes, once you hold GD1VS, you too will earn Dividends from other people depositing GOACT!

As soon as you have some GD1VS, you can use your Referral Link to bring others into the game. When they use your link, you get a bonus to your Dividends! Just the same, they can also refer others with their link - so you should refer with your link fast, far and wide!

Once you have a good bag of GD1VS, you'll want to start Compounding your Dividends - that is, rolling them into more GD1VS. This will have the effect of growing the size of your "portion of the pie", thus earning more Dividends, faster!

When it all falls apart on you - when you can't HODL your GD1VS anymore, it's okay to SELL them! When you do, you will receive GOACT to withdraw. The sale of GD1VS is also subject to a 10% fee, which also makes up part of the "Dividends" paid to those who still hold GD1VS.
