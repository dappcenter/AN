What is This?

Welcome!

ArcBank is a Smart Contract and Dapp deployed on the TRON Network, enabling you to buy and sell USDJ (USD Stablecoin) for TRX (TRON) at a minimal-fee rate. The contract is owned and manged by Arcadium Network Developer Team.

Arcadium Network Developer Team is the sole liquidity provider for the pool which enables trade - Every trade that is conducted (USDJ to TRX, and TRX to USDJ) incurs a 5% fee, to assist with exchange stability and administration.

ArcBank is built on the foundation of works by another developer, "Mr. F", who has recently created and published Stonk Fund. Please check it out, and join the @Stonk Simulator Discord Server for more!

The Smart Contract for "ArcBank" is a derivative work of This Contract by Mr. F - it is used with permission, and in this deployment has been modified to better suit Arcadium Network's purposes.
