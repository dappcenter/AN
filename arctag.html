What is an ArcTag?

An ArcTag is your username across the entire of Arcadium Network - the single piece of identity that is tied to your wallet address - your greatest online handle. Make it a good one!

Registering an ArcTag costs 10 TRX - a small fee to prevent namesquatting, which is collected by the Core Developer Team for maintenance of the platform.
